Sign Up for Postal Voting

This website makes it easier to apply for your postal vote.

  • You fill out this form.
  • We give you a pre-filled out form to print.
  • You sign it, and you post it to the local Electoral Registration Officer (we'll tell you the address).

They need to receive it at least 11 working days before the poll so:

  • 20th April - for May Elections (Send by 18th April)
  • 6th June - for EU Referendum (Send by 4th June)

You can only get a postal vote if you're already registered to vote - you can register to vote here. You need to register by 18th April to vote in the May Elections, or by the 7th June for the EU Referendum.

This is not an official website - you can download the form and fill it in yourself at aboutmyvote.co.uk

This form can't be used for Northern Ireland - go here instead.

Privacy: We don't store any of your personal details or generated forms.
